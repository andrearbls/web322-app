About

Hi there, my name is Andrea Robles, and I am currently enrolled in Computer Programming and Analysis at Seneca College, Newnham campus. I am in my third semester and so far I am really enjoying being in this program. I also graduated and have a diploma in Dental Hygiene from Oxford College of Business, Arts, and Technology. I am currently working part-time at a dental office as an office administrator/patient coordinator while studying full-time at the same time. Ever since I was in high school, I have always been interested in studying computers, however, most of my family members are in the dental career field that is why I got pressured to take a career that would fit in. During the time that I was working in the dental office, I realized that this career is not for me, so I took the big decision of changing my career by applying at Seneca College in order to get into the Computer Programming and Analysis program. I believe that if given a chance at obtaining something that will make us happy, it is worth the risk. Life is too short to live with regrets, and at this point, I can confidently say that I am happy with the decision I made in pursuing the Computer Programming and Analysis program.

During my free time, I do a lot of outdoor and indoor activities with my family and friends. For instance, when it is summertime I go hiking, fishing, and camping in different areas here in Ontario that I have not been to. I also like doing yoga and pilates in order to stay fit physically and mentally. In addition, I also want to share my favourite hobby during summer which is doing obstacle courses at Treetop Trekking. On the other hand, when it is wintertime I usually go skating and go to escape games in order to practice and enhance my problem-solving skills. Moreover, I also like going to the movie theatre to catch my favourite movie on the big screen.

My current goal in life is to successfully pass all my courses in my program while maintaining an excellent GPA record. After I graduate, I am aiming in getting into a reputable company where my training, knowledge, and skills will be an attribute and asset to the continual growth and development of my employer, as well as my co-workers. Once I establish myself, I want to become an inspiration to others and spread awareness that success has no timeline. If you believe in yourself, you will always do great things and find a way to achieve all your goals in life.
